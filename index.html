The Authentic French Croissant

The classic plain croissant is a simple pleasure, perfect when enjoyed with a cup of coffee or tea. Chocolate-filled croissants, known as "pain au chocolat," offer a delightful surprise with each bite, while almond croissants, or "croissants aux amandes," combine the nutty goodness of almonds with the pastry's buttery goodness. Savory versions filled with ham and cheese or other ingredients are equally delicious and make for a satisfying breakfast or snack.
